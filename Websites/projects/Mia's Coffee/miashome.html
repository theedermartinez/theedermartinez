Mia's Coffee

Located in Kinsale Ireland, welcome to Mia's Coffee where the air is thick with stories, and the aroma of freshly ground beans mingles with whispers of ancient magic. Wooden tables invite you to linger, while the hearth crackles with warmth. Sip your Finn McCool Espresso or indulge in warm scones – each bite a taste of Tir na nÓg. At Mia’s, time dances to fiddles, and every cup holds a touch of enchantment.

We are located in Kinsale, Ireland, however, do not fear, our coffee can be consumed worldwide.
