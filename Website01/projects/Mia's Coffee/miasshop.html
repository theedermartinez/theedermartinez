Mia's Spanish Coffee

Mia’s Spanish Coffee Dark Roast is a bold and intense coffee that captures the essence of rich, robust flavors. Roasted well beyond the second crack, these beans take on a nearly black color with a shiny surface from their natural oils. When brewed, this coffee reveals none of the green bean’s inherent characteristics; instead, it offers a dominant charred taste throughout. The thin body of Mia’s Spanish Coffee Dark Roast is a testament to its depth and complexity. If you crave a coffee that packs a punch and leaves a lasting impression, this dark roast won’t disappoint!

Mia's Colombian Coffee

Mia’s Colombian Coffee Medium Roast is a delightful symphony of flavors that will awaken your senses. Crafted from 100% Arabica beans, this medium roast strikes the perfect balance between richness and brightness. With each sip, you’ll encounter a harmonious blend of cocoa, nuts, and blackberry notes. The beans are grown in the majestic Andes mountain range, where altitude and climate contribute to their exceptional quality. Whether you’re a seasoned coffee connoisseur or an occasional sipper, Mia’s Colombian Medium Roast promises a bold, fruity, and satisfying experience in every cup.

Mia's Brazilian Coffee

Mia's Brazilian Dark Roast is known for its exquisite blend of premium beans from the Minas Gerais region. The beans are roasted to a deep brown color, releasing their rich aroma and flavor. The coffee has a full-bodied taste, with hints of chocolate, caramel, and nuts. It is smooth and balanced, with a low acidity and a lingering aftertaste. Mia's shops roasts the beans for over 24 hours, giving the beans enough time to adapt and develop their full potetial. Due to the complexity, this is the most expensive type of coffee we sell
